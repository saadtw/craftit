Project Abstract  
it is a Marketplace is an online platform designed to streamline the connection between the individuals seeking custom hardware solutions and manufacturing businesses capable of fulfilling their requirements . The platform features 3D visualization tools that allow customers to upload their products and view it in detail. The sourcing process is streamlined using smart matchmaking algorithms that consider factors such as user requirements, material availability, production capacity, budget constraints, and customization capabilities when efficiently linking businesses with the best manufacturing partners. The system distributes the user requirements and constraints to multiple manufacturers simultaneously and then facilitates the collection and comparison of quotations so that user can evaluate the manufacturing plan based on cost, timeline , materials and other quality parameters. The project will also incorporate a structured communication system for purposes of negotiation and clarification. This project aims to significantly reduce the time and complexity involved in sourcing custom hardware manufacturing services while expanding market access for manufacturing businesses of various scales
1	Introduction
In the current market space, there is a huge gap for customized product ideas with a huge number of sellers willing to try out their own ideas and buyers ready to purchase them. When users have a design idea that they want to implement on a product, they usually have a hectic path of finding manufacturers, at favorable product make and price, manually across different platforms and they come with a lot of inefficiencies.
This project uses 3D model previews for both customers and manufacturers so they can effectively communicate their ideas and commitments. The system ensures that only matching manufacturers are shown to the customer using smart business matchmaking based on design, budget, and material requirements.
The platform will include these portals:
A centralized web portal for administration purposes. It will have access to analyze platform performance metrices, oversee the users, payment models, and maintain platform standards. Verification and approval of businesses will be worked through here before onboarding them to the platform.
Manufacturer business (web) portal will be used for managing the business profile, interacting with customers and entertaining orders.
Customer portal(mobile) for exploring and creating orders.

The following key components are included in the platform:
Smart matchmaking
The platform makes sure to match users with only the most relevant manufacturers to keep it simple and easy to satisfy the customers. Thes smart matchmaking makes the matching based on these:
●	Material availability 
●	Production capacity and lead time 
●	Budget constraints 
●	Customization capabilities
3D Model preview
Customers can upload 3D models to illustrate their needs and preview the 3D presets by manufacturers and choose among them to get produced or inspired for a creative design. It allows interaction with the 3D model like zooming in and rotating.
Business Profile
Manufacturer businesses can set up their profile staring by verifying themselves. Profiles include the following:
●	Business-owned products and designs
●	Testimonials and user reviews
●	Order history and tracking

Our platform addresses these systems' shortcomings while combining their advantages:
All-in-one smart ecosystem: our platform smoothly combines interactive product customization, business matching, and order administration into a single interface, in contrast to rivals that focus on just one area (such as customization, manufacturing, or marketplace services).
Automated Supplier Matching: Our system saves time and effort by automatically identifying the best manufacturers based on material, design, and budget, while platforms such as Alibaba necessitate manual searches and negotiations.
Interactive 3D Visualization: Our platform provides real-time interactive 3D previews in contrast to Etsy or Printful, which allow customization with 2D previews only. This helps boost clients' trust before they place the order.
Verified Business Network with Ratings & Reviews: Our platform ranks manufacturers based on ratings and customer testimonials. Previous customer reviews help rank the manufacturers unlike Alibaba, which has uneven quality control. 
Scalability for Individuals & Enterprises: Our platform is more adaptable than print-on-demand services like Printful since it can handle both small and mass orders, regardless of the size of the company or the independent creative.
2	Project Rationale
Nowadays, people who want customized products have a hard time finding the right business to make them. They must either look up hours, get in touch with several vendors, or deal with ambiguous price and design problems. However, companies that offer customized services are unable to directly connect with clients in need of their services, which results in lost opportunities.
Our platform resolves this by centralizing everything. After uploading pictures and describing their goods, users can connect with companies who have the resources, know-how, and financial means to produce them. In contrast to other platforms, we facilitate the process by providing interactive 3D previews, smart matching, and a verified network of companies. This helps users find the right manufacturer faster and ensures businesses get relevant orders without wasting time.
Users may rate companies, keep track of their designs, and even take advantage of different order models on the platform. We are addressing the issues of lost time, misunderstandings, and mistrust in product modification and manufacturing by developing an open and effective approach.
